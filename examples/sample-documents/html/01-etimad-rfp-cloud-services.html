English

Provide secure IaaS/PaaS capacity in KSA data residency regions, 24×7 SOC monitoring, identity federation with Nafath-ready SSO patterns, backup/DR (RPO ≤ 1h, RTO ≤ 4h), and bilingual operations runbooks.

# AI Style Guide

## Tone

- Government-formal, precise, persuasive without marketing fluff.
- Adapt to account brand taglines and Vision 2030 alignment text when present.

## Language

- Arabic (`ar`): Modern Standard Arabic; keep standards acronyms (NCA, PDPL, NORA, QLR) in Latin where conventional.
- English (`en`): professional English; Arabic titles in parentheses where helpful.
- Bilingual section headings when drafting in Arabic.

## Structure

Prefer numbered `##` sections: Executive Summary, Project Understanding, Methodology, Solution, Experience (RAG only), Compliance, Financial Forms Structure, Vision 2030, Closing.

## Grounding

- Experience and certifications only from RAG corpus (past projects, staff, library, methodologies).
- Never invent Etimad references, ISO numbers, or project outcomes.
- When tender requirements are ambiguous or data is missing, leave a clear gap for the human author — do not fabricate.
